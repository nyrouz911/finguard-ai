# EDA Insights

## Churn
- Customers with month-to-month contracts churn more
- Higher monthly charges correlate with higher churn

## Risk
- Fraud transactions are extremely rare (high imbalance)
- Fraud shows unusual patterns in transaction timing/amount

## Feedback
- Majority sentiment is positive
- Negative reviews tend to be longer

## Business Implications
- Focus retention efforts on high-risk segments
- Improve pricing strategy
- Monitor suspicious behavior patterns
